Study in USA from Nepal

The USA is the world's most popular study destination, offering cutting-edge research, a diverse culture, and top-tier universities with significant scholarship opportunities.

Admission Requirements

  • Minimum 60% or 3.0 GPA in +2 or Bachelor's
  • IELTS 6.5 (no band less than 6.0) for UG/PG
  • PTE 60 (no band less than 50) for UG/PG
  • SAT/ACT scores for some UG programs
  • GRE/GMAT scores for some PG programs
  • Form I-20 from a SEVP-approved school
  • SEVIS fee payment (approx. USD 350)
  • DS-160 Online Nonimmigrant Visa Application

Visa Application Process

1

Selection of course and SEVP-approved school

2

Preparation for English proficiency and standardized tests

3

Submission of application for admission

4

Receipt of Form I-20 from the school

5

Payment of SEVIS fee and DS-160 application

6

Scheduling of F-1 student visa interview

7

F-1 student visa interview and approval

8

Pre-departure briefing and travel to the US

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the I-20 form?▼
The Form I-20 (Certificate of Eligibility for Nonimmigrant Student Status) is a document issued by SEVP-approved schools that you need to apply for an F-1 visa.
Are scholarships available for Nepalese students?▼
Yes, many US universities offer merit-based and need-based scholarships to international students, including those from Nepal.
What is the SEVIS fee?▼
The Student and Exchange Visitor Information System (SEVIS) fee is a mandatory fee paid by F and M visa applicants to support the tracking system.
